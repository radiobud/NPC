Using selectors to work with site code

A user lookup has multiple key objects: the account age/info, the shield, pets, trophies. We can't remove these! But you can, for example, change the background of those areas to your own theme. Or select a specific pet and overlay custom art (that does not mis-represent what it is!).

Most of the Neo elements use IDs or classes, so you need to select those in your CSS. How to do this?

"Manifesto"

I want this to be a middle ground for people who want to make something of their page, but who don't want to drop in templates - or who can't find templates that work for them. When it comes to coding, most people learn by doing - piece by piece, trying out new tools and skills. I (personally!) find it overwhelming to try to review code in one big chunk, unless it's my own. Even then! Comments help. Good formatting helps. Frequent checks (debugs) help. Dev tools.
