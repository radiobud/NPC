How to Use

To customize your sidebar with these icons, follow these steps:

  1. Copy the direct URL to an image. (Right-click the image, then 'copy link address'.)
  2. Go to your "Customise" page (the link is also at the top of your right-hand sidebar on any normal NPC page.)
  3. Under "Quick Links," scroll to the bottom to add your own.
  4. For Icon, select Custom Icon URL.
  5. Paste in the URL you copied.
  6. Make sure you grab the URL to whatever page on NPC you're trying to link to, too!
  7. Once you title the link and save, you should see the mini-icon appear under your Quick Links!
