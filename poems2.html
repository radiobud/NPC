Doctor, Death (For One Techo)

for Techo Day 2025

how brittle it is,
the way greetings
morph into goodbyes,
the way the days languish
beside another and another
letting-go.

somehow you still start with a smile.
(scowling does not stop them all.)

how broken it is,
being this kind of icon:
beside you flows joy after joy
out the door,
but you are the guide
to the leaving-behind.

your smile
is only for the pets.

they have done
nothing
wrong.

More Than Dessert

for Chocolate Ball 2025

A single offered chocolate
to a hungry pet

surpasses any sweetness with
its versatile context.

The rare and darkest chocolates
pamper as a treat;

whip-topped cocoa in the winter:
refreshing, welcome heat.

Quick snap of just a bite-size bar
will fuel them to explore.

Sculpted seashell chocolates?
Tasteful, tastier decor.

And to intimidate a Chocolate pet,
put yourself into their shoes.

What "treat" would make you most concerned?
A chocolate shaped like you.

Gratitude with Reservations

for Faerie Festival 2025


Faeries, air and of garden,
Dark, fountain and grey,
I will gladly help find you
Any item you say.


But please, faeries gentle,
Who beg for plushie fire lizards
Consider your timing:
I kinda need the shop wizard.

The Bori Who Fixes

for Bori Day 2025

It's like this: start with a fishing pole,
Broken coins, a ripped-up Fuzzle;
But Donny's claws are steady
With the solving of each puzzle.

Some thread to stitch a little tear,
Help fix, repair, restore.
I watch with awe, and ask him next:
Can I come back with more?

Go find another broken thing,
On quests, or fished, one at a time.
Thank the Bori who connects all 3: the fragments,
you, and the first word of each line.
